Juan is graphic designer, photographer and artist and began his business - Creating Destiny Graphics in 2008. He has done work for various restaurants, ministries, political figures, and non-profits. Some of his most recent work was done for Keller Williams Heritage and Poblanos Mexican Restaurant and Catering. He is currently enrolled as a student at Northwest Vista College and will graduate this coming fall with an Associates of Applied Science in Digital Media.

Juan is also an ordained minister and is presently the lead elder of Talitha Cumi Ministries in San Antonio, Texas. His main focus is teaching about the roots of our faith in our Messiah Yeshua (Jesus Christ). Talitha Cumi is a house church ministry that meets regularly on the first and third Friday of each month. Talitha Cumi believes in flowing in the gifts of the Ruach Hakodesh (Holy Spirit), honoring and keeping the sabbath and the Feasts of our Lord.
